About This Portal

This portal helps students interact with the university administration through a secure and organized system.

Students can register online and access their personal dashboard after their account is approved by the administration.

The platform keeps academic information organized so students can easily follow their progress during their session.

The system provides a structured environment for managing academic records and university activities.
